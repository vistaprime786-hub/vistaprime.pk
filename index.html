📍 Location Advantages

  • Main entrance: Multan Public School Road (MPS Road)
  • Nishtar Hospital se qareeb — sahulat aam hai
  • Multan Airport sirf 15 minute ki doori par
  • Top schools, colleges aur shopping centers paas mein
  • Society ke andar Grand Mosque maujood hai
