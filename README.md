# Vesta Prime – Multan Real Estate Website

**Vesta Prime** is a luxury real estate website for Multan's premier Royal Orchard property agency. The site targets both local and overseas Pakistani clients with an international-standard design featuring a black & gold luxury theme.

## ✅ Completed Features

### Design & UX
- ✅ Black & gold luxury theme (matching reference site style)
- ✅ Playfair Display serif + Inter sans-serif typography
- ✅ Animated preloader with Vesta Prime logo
- ✅ Smooth scroll & scroll-reveal animations
- ✅ Cursor glow effect (desktop only)
- ✅ Fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop)

### Navigation
- ✅ Fixed sticky navbar with scroll effect
- ✅ Active section highlighting
- ✅ Mobile hamburger menu with slide-in drawer

- ✅ **Hero** – Full-screen auto-sliding background with stats counter
- ✅ **About** – Company intro with floating badge & features

- ✅ **Royal Orchard** – Investment Options + Plot Sizes + Location Map
- ✅ **Team** – CEO Khawar Nadeem & Co-Founder Dr. Abdul Basit
- ✅ **Overseas Section** – Targeted messaging for overseas Pakistanis
- ✅ **Contact** – Contact cards + WhatsApp form submission
